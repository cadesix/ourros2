OURROS FILMS

We aim to take small bets on big ideas, testing concepts that we believe have huge upside via strong IP. The content landscape has changed drastically in the past 5 years; multimillion dollar properties can be built on the backs of small teams and creators who can leverage short-form. We’ve proven that we’re best positioned to navigate this landscape and have created a process to test concepts, build worlds, and develop IP across numerous platforms. Here’s our roadmap.‍

Proof of Concept

We identify emerging directors and fund the development of scripts that we believe in, producing a short film and pitching it alongside a feature script and short-form campaign.

Demonstrate Demand

We debut characters, concepts, and storylines on TikTok with the potential to reach millions of eyeballs organically; no ad spend, no production budget, just storytelling. This serves multiple purposes: in testing concepts via short-form we begin to build our world in bite-sized chunks, creating the first touchpoint for future fans while also building a distribution channel for any product around the strength of the IP.

Develop IP

Landing a feature signals success and is a huge win for everyone involved, but the ultimate goal is to build a world around the IP. Today’s consumers (esp Gen-Z) are faced with an endless sea of content and products; it’s increasingly difficult to cut through the noise. Success in today’s content landscape means building a dedicated base of fans who are willing to invest time and money into the world we build. Our journey doesn’t end at the box office; once we’ve established a property, the opportunities to build into tangential verticals are limitless.

North Star

Our vision for the future of content is comprehensive worldbuilding with multiple cross-platform touchpoints. We care about good stories and the art of filmmaking, and recognize that the landscape is changing. We know as well as anyone else that attention is increasingly being taken away from the big screen towards vertical short-form, and have proven time and again that we’re able to leverage that attention to build fandom.
